Sharda Minority Convent

Sharda Minority Convent is a co-educational institution committed to providing quality education with a balance of academic excellence and value-based learning.

We focus on developing each child’s intellectual, emotional, social, and moral strengths. Regular classes, activities, and guidance help them build confidence and discipline.

Our teachers work with dedication so that every student can understand concepts clearly and grow into a responsible and kind human being.

“Education with discipline and values is the core of Sharda Minority Convent.”
