Admission Procedure

Simple and clear process to join Sharda Minority Convent.

Step 1: Visit School / Enquiry

Parents may visit the school office to know about classes, facilities, and fee details.

Step 2: Collect & Fill Form

Collect the admission form from the office and fill in all required details.

Step 3: Submit Documents

Submit the form along with necessary documents for verification.

Step 4: Interaction / Test

For certain classes, a short interaction or basic test may be conducted.

Step 5: Confirm Admission

On approval, pay the fees to confirm the admission.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate of the child
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Address Proof (Aadhaar / Ration Card etc.)
  • Previous school Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Previous class report card (for higher classes)
