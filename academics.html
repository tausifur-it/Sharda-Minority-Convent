Academic Structure

Our curriculum is designed to build a strong foundation in all subjects.

Pre-Primary

Nursery, LKG, UKG with play-based learning, rhymes, stories, and basic literacy.

Primary (I–V)

Focus on English, Mathematics, EVS, basic Science, and regular activities.

Middle (VI–VIII)

Science, Mathematics, Social Studies, Computer, and project-based learning.

Secondary (IX–X)

Board-focused preparation with conceptual clarity and regular tests.

Teaching Methods

Interactive teaching, doubt-clearing sessions, assignments, and oral activities.

Beyond Classroom

Quizzes, competitions, moral education, and co-curricular activities.
